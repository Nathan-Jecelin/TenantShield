# TenantShield — Feature Ideas

## High Impact — Growth & Engagement
- [ ] Email newsletter signup — capture visitors, notify about new blog posts/features
- [ ] "Claim this property" for landlords — let property managers respond to reviews
- [ ] Saved/bookmarked addresses — let logged-in users save addresses and get notified of new violations

## SEO & Traffic
- [ ] More blog content — "Top 10 worst buildings in [neighborhood]", "How to break a lease in Chicago"
- [ ] Neighborhood landing pages — dedicated pages per neighborhood with aggregated violation stats
- [ ] FAQ page — common tenant rights questions to capture "People Also Ask" search traffic

## Trust & Credibility
- [ ] Review verification badges — mark reviews from verified tenants
- [ ] "Report this review" button — maintain review quality
- [ ] Review response from landlords — two-sided reviews build credibility

## User Experience
- [ ] Map view — show violations/complaints on a map for a searched area
- [ ] Comparison tool — compare two addresses side-by-side
- [ ] PDF report download — let users download a building report to share

## Monetization (when ready)
- [ ] Featured listings for landlords — landlords with good scores pay to be highlighted
- [ ] Premium reports — detailed historical data or trend analysis
